How does this work?

Basically, you just have to key in your grades,
afterwards, we will calculate your GPA.
Then we will tell you which module is pulling your
GPA down currently, based on your grades. For
example, you are getting a C+ grade in a 4
credit module, but a C grade in a 2 credit module.
The page will tell you that the 4 credit module is
more important.

Please do not key in Pass/Fail modules as
they do not affect your GPA.
