Stop Procrastination

1. Break your work into little steps.
Part of the reason why we procrastinate is because subconsciously, we find the work too overwhelming for us.
Break your work into little parts, this way you can focus on one thing at a time.

If you're still procrastinating, then break down the work even more.
Eventually you'll find it easy to be able to complete.

2. Eliminate things that cause you to procrastinate.
If you are procrastinating too much, there is probably something distracting you from your work. You should identify what is causing you to procrastinate.

Afterwards, you should avoid what is causing you to procrastinate. If it is a website, an app or anything related to your phone or computer, put them aside while you do your work.
