Multi-tasking

1. Define the Tasks in Advance
Always be sure of what you are going to do before you do it. Don't wait until you are in the middle of your first task then start to decide what you want to accomplish.
Have a plan and set your goals, even when you're multitasking, keep the amount of things you want to multitask small so you would not have too much on your hands at one time.
To be efficient, you should already have the tasks you want to accomplish set and stick to them. If you think of additional stuffs, you can always add it to another list.

2. Recognise what can or can't be multitasked.
There are going to be tasks that will require your complete attention. Some tasks do not work well with multitasking.
You must recognise those tasks and avoid multitasking with them, if you do, the results may turn out worse than you predicted
even though it may save you some time.
